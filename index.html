The Microchip.

Smaller than your fingernail. Without it — your phone is a brick, your car won't start, your console is dead. It's the brain of every modern device.

What IS a chip? 🧠

Tap each panel to reveal the answer!

How big is a chip?

About the size of your fingernail — but packed with 15 BILLION microscopic switches! (比指甲還小，但有150億個開關！)

👆 Tap to reveal

What does it do?

It's billions of tiny ON/OFF switches (transistors). ON = 1, OFF = 0. Combine billions of 1s and 0s = everything your phone can do! (開=1，關=0，組合起來就是一切！)

👆 Tap to reveal

What is it made of?

Silicon — the same element found in ordinary sand! Scientists learned to purify it and build circuits on it. The whole world runs on glorified sand! (世界靠提純的沙子運轉！)

👆 Tap to reveal

Who uses chips?

Every smartphone, car, game console, hospital machine, satellite, and AI chatbot. No chip = none of these exist. The entire modern world depends on them! (沒有晶片，現代世界就消失！)
